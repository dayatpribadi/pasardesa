Kunjungan Bupati Ogan Ilir Di Pasar Tradisional

Organic farming relies on natural methods like composting, crop rotation, and biological pest control to maintain soil health and manage pests. This means no synthetic chemicals are used in the cultivation process.

Diversity is a cornerstone of our farming philosophy. We carefully select a wide range of crops, ensuring a varied and balanced ecosystem. This not only helps to naturally deter pests and diseases but also promotes soil health by varying the nutrient demands of different plants. Crop rotation further enhances the soil's fertility by preventing the depletion of specific nutrients. This strategic planning ensures that our fields remain productive and vibrant year after year. By following these three steps, we cultivate a sustainable, thriving organic farm that produces high-quality, nutrient-dense produce while respecting and preserving the natural environment. This holistic approach not only benefits the health of our customers but also contributes to the overall well-being of the planet.

At our organic farm, we begin with the foundation of healthy, nutrient-rich soil. We employ sustainable practices such as crop rotation, cover cropping, and composting. This helps maintain soil fertility, structure, and microbial diversity. By avoiding synthetic fertilizers and pesticides, we allow natural processes to flourish, creating a balanced ecosystem where plants can thrive.

Pasar Desa adalah platform digital berbasis website yang dirancang khusus untuk memfasilitasi desa-desa di Kabupaten Ogan Ilir dalam mempromosikan dan menjual produk-produk lokal unggulan. Aplikasi ini menjadi jembatan antara pelaku UMKM desa, pengrajin, petani, nelayan, dan masyarakat umum dengan pasar yang lebih luas, baik di tingkat kabupaten maupun nasional.
